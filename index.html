Hi, I am Dalilah L. Earley!

My Story

Born and raised in Chicago, my wanderlust began the moment I graduated from high school. Though I loved my city, I needed to get out and explore. I decided to attend Drake University in Iowa, where I doubled majored in Computer Science and English. During my time there, I became an Resident Assistant, and after 2.5 years in the position, I grew more than I could have ever imagined.

Before my time at Drake University ended, I decided to travel to London. It only took a few short weeks for me to fall in love with the city's diverse food markets, museums, theatres. In my spare time, I visited Shakespeare's hometown (Stratford-upon-Avon), Stonehenge, Paris, and Rome. I was even able to squeeze in a trip to visit Dublin, Ireland - one of the homes of my Earley Ancestors.

Eventually, my travels came to an end and I returned home to Chicago. In Fall 2018, I enrolled into the Information Technology and Management masters program at the Illinois Institue of Technology.
